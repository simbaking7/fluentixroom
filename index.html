About Fluentix Room

Fluentix Room is a dynamic online learning platform dedicated to helping students succeed academically and communicate confidently across languages. Founded in 2026, we offer three core learning pathways designed to meet the needs of diverse learners. Our English programme (TEFL) is specially designed for non-English speakers who want to build strong communication skills for school, work, and everyday life. We focus on practical, interactive lessons that develop speaking, listening, reading, and writing in a supportive environment. In addition, we provide academic tutoring for Grades 4 to 11, helping students strengthen their understanding of key school subjects, improve performance, and gain confidence in their studies. Our tutoring is aligned with school curricula and tailored to each learner’s pace and goals. We also offer Portuguese classes for non-Portuguese speakers, ideal for beginners and intermediate learners who want to communicate effectively in real-life situations, whether for travel, school, or personal growth. At Fluentix, we believe that every student learns differently. That’s why our teaching approach is student-centered, engaging, and focused on real results. We are committed to creating a positive online learning experience where students feel supported, motivated, and empowered to succeed. Learn a language. Strengthen your knowledge. Unlock your future.
